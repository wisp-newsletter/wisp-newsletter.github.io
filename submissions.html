Submissions

How do I submit?

We heavily encourage submissions which go along with the guidelines outlined below. We encourage you, if you are considering writing for us, to message us on our Instagram. This gives us a rough estimate of numbers, and allows us to help you out if you need it!
Our preference for receiving articles is to have the Google document shared with our email address, however we also accept PDFs sent to our email.

Submissions Guidelines

Submissions will fall into one of the following categories: Review article about a method, review article about a recent breakthrough, spotlight article in a past or present inspiring woman in STEM.

MUSTS:

- Title and your name clearly labelled at the top of the document
- In-text citations and reference list/bibliography. We know this can be scary if you aren't used to citations, but just ask and we can help!
- Female-written.. it is the Women In STEM Paper!
- Minimum of 11pt font

Not necessary, but appreciated

- Easy to understand. Please define jargon either in text or as a footnote
- Colours, images, and diagrams!
- Formatting; change your font to a serif or sans-serif font and format headings etc.

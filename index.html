About WISP

The Women In STEM Paper (WISP) is a newsletter written by girls in STEM for girls in STEM.
Our monthly publication includes articles written by aspiring Women in STEM about a niche field that interests them.
This allows contributors to develop skills in researching their topic, and also in academic writing.
